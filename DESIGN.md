# Design System for Yasmine Cortier

## Core Identity
Yasmine Cortier is an electric R&B/Soul singer, DJ, and performer known for her magnetic presence ("Freak Magnet"). Her brand represents premium nightlife energy, soulful connection, and vibrant community engagement.

## Visual Language
- **Theme:** Dark Mode by default. It should feel like a premium, sleek nightlife experience.
- **Color Palette:**
  - **Primary:** Deep Electric Violet (e.g., #6B21A8, #8B5CF6) - represents her soulful R&B vibe and magnetic personality.
  - **Secondary:** Neon Pink/Magenta (e.g., #D946EF) - representing her vibrant, high-energy Karaoke and DJ hosting.
  - **Background:** Deep Space Black / Dark Charcoal (#0F172A) for a stark, high-contrast, premium look.
  - **Text:** Crisp White (#F8FAFC) for primary text, soft gray/lavender for secondary text.
- **Typography:**
  - Headers: Bold, modern, slightly geometric (e.g., 'Outfit', 'Montserrat', or 'Inter' with bold weights).
  - Body: Clean, readable sans-serif (e.g., 'Inter' or 'Roboto').
- **Effects:**
  - Glassmorphism on cards and music players.
  - Smooth gradients and subtle neon glows around interactive elements to draw the user in.
  - Hover animations to make the site feel "alive" and interactive.

## Layout Concepts
- **Hero Section:** Full-screen hero, deep dynamic gradient, bold text "YASMINE CORTIER", with a quick summary "Singer • DJ • Entertainer" and a primary Call to Action (e.g., "Listen to Freak Magnet").
- **Music & Projects:** A grid or carousel showcasing her 2011 album *Freak Magnet*, with tracks like "Chance (Na Ham)" and "Solace". Must look like a modern music player/portfolio.
- **History & Timeline:** A vertical timeline showcasing her career from emceeing and hosting at *The Merq* and *Volcano's Party Island* to her present-day endeavors.
- **Booking/Contact:** A sleek, glowing form at the bottom for users to connect and book her for future gigs.
- **Social Links:** Prominent, beautifully styled social and music platform icons.
